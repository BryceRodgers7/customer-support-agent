Company Overview
Protis designs and manufactures premium consumer electronics focused on audio fidelity, visual accuracy, and professional-grade build quality. Products are positioned for enthusiasts, creatives, and professionals. Customers generally expect white-glove support and fast resolution.

PRODUCT CATALOG

1. Protis Aether X1 – Wireless Headphones
Category: Audio / Headphones
Price: $399
Product Overview
The Aether X1 are flagship wireless over-ear headphones featuring adaptive ANC, spatial audio, and studio-tuned drivers.
Service Plan Options
Standard Warranty: 1 year
Protis Care+ ($59/year):
Accidental damage coverage
Battery replacement after 24 months
Priority support queue
Setup Guide
Charge headphones fully (USB-C).
Hold power button for 3 seconds.
Open Bluetooth settings on device.
Select “Protis Aether X1”.
Install Protis Control App for firmware updates.
FAQs
Q: Can I use them wired?
A: Yes, via included 3.5mm cable (ANC disabled in wired mode).
Q: Do they support multipoint Bluetooth?
A: Yes, up to two devices.
Q: Are they water resistant?
A: No, not rated for water exposure.
Troubleshooting
No sound after pairing:
Reset headphones (hold power + volume down for 10s).
ANC not working:
Ensure firmware is updated via the app.
Known Limitations
ANC disabled in wired mode
Not designed for workouts or moisture exposure
